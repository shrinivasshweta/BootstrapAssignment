the urban artisan

The Urban Artisan was started with a simple mission: to inspire through the power of the written word. The result is the original and incomparable style of The Urban Artisan. This remains the often imitated, never replicated look of the line to this day. what started as 48 black+white cards has grown into a full and flourishing range of cards.

The Urban Artisan appeals to an incredibly diverse demographic. Our products transcend the traditional boundaries of age, gender, and culture by appealing to people who expect more out of a greeting card: people who want to give and receive something meaningful.
